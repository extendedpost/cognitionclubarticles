Self-Care Ideas

This list is to help you unwind and rejuvenate.


  • Schedule a Massage and a dinner or coffee after.
  • Go out to a nice restaurant and enjoy a meal at a bar type restaurant.
  • Take a long hot shower
  • Start a cold shower regiment
  • Listen to music and pace around (or dance)
  • Learn a new instrument
  • Take up a new hobby
  • Go to Barnes and Noble and read some magazines
  • Buy someone a gift (this is fun)
  • Splurge and buy yourself a gift
  • Do some research and find a medical professional
  • Write
  • Take a drive and explorer somewhere new for the day
  • Use a heating pad on your back and relax
  • Learn a language
  • Listen to a foreign language podcast
  • Call a friend
  • Go out to dinner with a friend
  • Sneak out to a comedy club
  • Make yourself some easy meals
  • Start a book club
  • Find an online course and do it with a friend
  • Take a walk for 10 minutes
  • Stretch
  • Buy yourself a nice new piece of clothing
  • Buy some new undergarmets and socks
  • Join a meetup or Start one
  • Listen to an audiobook or podcast
  • Take a nap and restart the day with a coffee
  • Hydrate, try to drink half your body in oz of water (Thanks, TB12)
  • Practice the breathing exercise
  • Turn off the lights and light up some candles
  • Do nothing and enjoy it
