31 Self-Care Ideas

This list is to help you unwind and rejuvenate. The goal is to reinvest, laugh, and return to balance.

  • 1. Schedule a Massage and a dinner or coffee after.
  • 2. Go out to a nice restaurant and enjoy a meal at a bar type restaurant so you won't feel awkward eating at a table by yourself.
  • 3. Take a long hot shower
  • 4. Listen to music and pace around (or dance)
  • 5. Learn a new instrument
  • 6. Take up a new hobby
  • 7. Go to Barnes and Noble and read some magazines
  • 8. Buy someone a gift (this is fun)
  • 9. Splurge and buy yourself a gift
  • 10. Do some research and find a medical professional
  • 11. Write
  • 12. Take a drive and explorer somewhere new for the day
  • 13. Use a heating pad on your back and relax
  • 14. Learn a language
  • 15. Listen to a foreign language podcast
  • 16. Call a friend
  • 17. Go out to dinner with a friend
  • 18. Sneak out to a comedy club
  • 19. Make yourself some easy meals
  • 20. Start a book club
  • 21. Find an online course and do it with a friend
  • 22. Take a walk for 10 minutes
  • 23. Stretch
  • 24. Buy yourself a nice new piece of clothing
  • 25. Buy some new undergarmets and socks
  • 26. Join a meetup or Start one
  • 27. Listen to an audiobook or podcast
  • 28. Take a nap and restart the day with a coffee
  • 29. Hydrate, try to drink half your body in oz of water (Thanks, TB12)
  • 30. Practice the breathing exercise for 10 minutes where you take a deep breath in, and hold for 3 seconds and breath out.
  • 31. Turn off the lights and light up some candles
  • 32. Do nothing all day and enjoy it
